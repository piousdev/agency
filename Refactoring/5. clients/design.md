## 1. Design Overview

### 1.1 Purpose of This Document

This document describes the structural design, layout, and feature organization of the Clients navigation without specifying visual styling, colors, or specific implementation details.

### 1.2 Design Principles

- **Clarity**: Quick access to client information
- **Completeness**: Full 360Â° view of client relationships
- **Context**: All relevant information in one place
- **Efficiency**: Minimal clicks to common actions
- **Flexibility**: Multiple views for different workflows
- **Intelligence**: Proactive alerts and insights
