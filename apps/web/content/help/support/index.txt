### Email support

For technical support, contact our team:

- **General inquiries**: support@docs-marketing.com
- **Technical support**: api-support@docs-marketing.com
- **Security issues**: security@docs-marketing.com

### Response times

- **Critical issues**: 2-4 hours
- **High priority**: 24 hours
- **Standard requests**: 2-3 business days

## Reporting issues

When reporting an issue, please include:

1. Your account ID or API key (redact sensitive parts)
2. Complete error messages
3. Request/response examples
4. Steps to reproduce the issue
5. Expected vs actual behavior
